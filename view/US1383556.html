Application filed September 18, 1920. Serial No. 411,303.

To all whom it may concern Be it known that I, RUDOLF Merlin, a

citizen of the German Republic, and residing at Solingen, Germany, have invented certain new and useful Improvements in Nail-Scissors, of which the following is a specification.

This invention relates to improvements in nail-scissors, and more particularly to a combination with the latter of a nail-cleaning instrument in such a way that when not in use the latter can be placed with a shell forming its handle over the sharp ends of the cuttingmembers of the pair of scissors while it engages with its other end in a lug provided on one of said out ting-members, said shell forming thus a guard for thesharp ends of said cuttingmembers, while the nailcleaning instrument can be easily detached for intended use of both the latter and said pair of nail-scissors.
